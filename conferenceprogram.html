Texas Applied Mathematics and Engineering Symposium

The Texas Applied Mathematics and Engineering Symposium is an applied mathematics, engineering and computer science conference to be held at The University of Texas at Austin's Institute for Computational and Engineering Science (ICES) on September 21 - 23, 2017. The focus of the conference is to showcase research in applied math and computing sciences both for established academics in the field as well as current students. A call for abstracts and registration will eventually come, and anyone is welcome to submit and attend. This event is sponsored by ICES.
