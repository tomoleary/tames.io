  • Registration Information

    Register at the Eventbrite page here


    Early registration deadline: August 21 2017, Fee $40.


    Late registration deadline: September 21, 2017, Fee $50.


  • Travel Awards

    Thanks to the SIAM Texas-Louisiana (TX-LA) Section, there will be 10 travel awards worth $220 for student attendants outside of the Austin area. The conference registration fees will also be waived for all award winners. In order to qualify, applicants must apply to participate in the conference through a talk or a poster.

    Please send CVs and abstracts, as well as a brief expression of interest in the
    award to tames@ices.utexas.edu by Monday, August 7th, 2017.

  • Poster Prize

    In collaboration with the SIAM TX-LA Section, we will be offering $500 in cash prizes for junior researchers in the poster session. Applicants who would like to speak as well as present a poster are invited to do both.

  • Travel and Lodging

    If you are flying to Austin, you will want to use the Austin-Bergstrom International Airport (AUS).
    Two popular B&B's near campus are the Star of Texas Inn and the Austin Folk House.
